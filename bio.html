Processing Frog Reintroduction Data at Mt. Auburn Cemetery | August 2019 - December 2019

I have worked with Joe Martinez from the Harvard Museum of Comparative Zoology to help monitor frog reintroduction at Mt. Auburn Cemetery by analyzing frog call recordings.

Advocacy Against Rattlesnake Roundups | November 2018 - Present

You can view an essay I wrote about the topic here, and a YouTube video showing the time-lapse process of creating a design advocating for the termination of roundups here.

I am currently working with the Advocates for Snake Preservation (ASP) as a volunteer designer to help create an eco-friendly, online, print-on-demand t-shirt store to serve as a passive fundraiser. My designs reflect the themes in their "Living With Snakes" and "Stop Snake Slaughter at Rattlesnake Roundups" campaigns. These designs are used to create merchandise that helps fund their research.

Waquoit Bay | August 2016 - Present

I have participated in Waquoit Bay Reserve's TIDAL Quest program several summers in a row (2016-2019), collecting water quality data for the "Bringing Wetlands to Market" project, conducting ecological surveys, and studying the natural history of the bay and its estuaries.

Currently working with Dr. Jordan Mora and other staff at the Waquoit Bay National Estuarine Research Reserve (WBNERR) to "translate" their data into graphics, brochures, and other resources to help communicate their water quality data to the public.

Managing a small team of a graphic designer and two programmers who are also working on this project and creating exhibit resources for the visitor center at the Waquoit Bay Research Reserve.

Other | April 2018 - Present

Since April 2018, I have been documenting sightings of local wildlife. These sightings are uploaded to iNaturalist, an online community of naturalists that contribute to research by recording sightings of wildlife.

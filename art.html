Artwork and Design

Projects and experience related to art and design.

Tale of Scales | September 2019 - Present

One of my current projects is the Tale of Scales deck of playing cards. It started as an assignment for Graphic Design class, however, I decided to turn to crowdfunding to get them printed.

The deck features 56 unique reptiles and amphibians, 55 illustrations, and 54 cards. The Kickstarter page can be found here, and received over $6,000 in preorders from over 200 backers in countries all over the world.
I've been selling extra decks and donating portions of the profits to wildlife conservation and education efforts. You can see Tale of Scale's website here.

Contract UI Designer for Artix Entertainment | July 2020 - September 2020

Created icon art and UI revisions for the game AdventureQuest 3D, created by Artix Entertainment.

Resource Creation for WBNERR | June 2020 - August 2020

Worked with Dr. Jordan Mora and other staff at the Waquoit Bay National Estuarine Research Reserve (WBNERR) to translate their data into graphics, brochures, and other resources to help communicate their water quality data to the public.

Also helped create interactive programs to run on the computers in their visitor center.

Featured Roll20 Creator | June 2021

My pixel art collection, Sorcerous Sprites, was featured in the Roll20 reserve and distributed to subscription users.

Graphics for the Advocates for Snake Preservation (ASP) | July 2019 - August 2020

I have worked with the Advocates for Snake Preservation (ASP) as a volunteer designer to help create an eco-friendly, online, print-on-demand t-shirt store to serve as a passive fundraiser.

My designs reflect the themes in their "Living With Snakes" and "Stop Snake Slaughter at Rattlesnake Roundups" campaigns. These designs are used to create merchandise that helps fund their research.

You can view my designs here, and the shop here.

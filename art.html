I run a YouTube channel under the name "Haasio Draws" that has a following of 14,000 subscribers. I post art, drawing tutorials and animations, sometimes under environmental or herpetological activism. In addition to my YouTube channel, I also have a DeviantArt account, where I do commissioned work and post studies and practices. I occasionally host art contests on DeviantArt to spread awareness about Climate Change and inspire my audience to send messages though their artwork.

My preferred medium is digital, though I also create acrylic paintings. The majority of these are donated to auction fundraisers hosted by the Waquoit Bay Junior Sailing Program or the nonprofit reptile rescue, The Gecko Sanctuary.

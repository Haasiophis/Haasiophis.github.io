Emma Hsiao
Hi!
I'm a senior at Weston High School, Massachusetts, with a passion for design, wildlife conservation, and marine biology.

I hope to apply my artistic talents to the scientific field to aid in communication of data and general awareness. My artwork can be seen on Tale of Scales, the Advocates for Snake Preservation's online shop, and on the 2020 species description paper for Ramphotyphlops mollyozakiae, or Ozaki's blindsnake.
Internships


Scientific Illustration | January 2020 - Present

I'm working with Van Wallach from the Harvard Museum of Comparative Zoology as a scientific illustrator to create visuals for some of his publications.

Pictured are illustrations of Ramphotyphlops mollyozakiae. The paper can be found here.


Processing Frog Reintroduction Data | August 2019 - Present

I have worked with Joe Martinez from the Harvard Museum of Comparative Zoology to help monitor frog reintroduction at Mt. Auburn Cemetery by analyzing frog call recordings using Kaleidoscope, a sound analysis program.



Projects


Tale of Scales | September 2019 - Present

I illustrated and designed a deck of reptile and amphibian themed playing cards in light of global amphibian decline and to shed light on the diversity of herpetofauna. This project was 50% funded in its first 24 hours on Kickstarter. The project hit over $5,000 total, with over 200 backers.

I have been selling extra decks and donating portions of the profits to wildlife conservation and education efforts such as the Advocates for Snake Preservation and AXOLOTITLÁN.

Tale of Scales has won an award at the Massachusetts Art Education Association Emerging Artists Exhibition (Virginia M. Diani-Adams Award) and recieved a Gold Key from the Scholastic Art and Writing Awards.

This project is constantly evolving, and I am currently working on bringing these playing cards to physical vendors and retail.

Contract UI Designer for Artix Entertainment | July 2020 - September 2020

Created icon art and UI revisions for the game AdventureQuest 3D, created by Artix Entertainment.

Graphic Design for Snake Conservation | July 2019 - Present

I am a volunteer designer for the Advocates for Snake Preservation (ASP) and creating a series of illustrations to reflect the themes of their “Living with Snakes” and “Stop Snake Slaughter at Rattlesnake Roundups” campaigns. These designs are used to create merchandise that helps fund their research.

Art YouTube Channel | December 2014 - Present

I run a YouTube channel with an audience of 17 thousand subscribers under the name 'Haasio Draws', where I draw and animate, sometimes as an environmental or herpetological activist. I also create drawing tutorials, critique videos, and animated shorts.
